About Me

Growing up in small town Poughkeepsie, NY, there was not much to do. When I was around 7 years old, my dad connected his old VCR camcorder to my television set. That was when my next door neighbor and I started using our creativity to record and make movies. To this day, I believe this was what inspired me to pursue a career in the entertainment industry.

My next door neighbor and me

After graduating from Ithaca College with a BS degree in Television-Radio and a minor in Finance, I moved out to Los Angeles. I am currently working as a Supervisor at OMG23, a media agency dedicated to the Disney/Fox Theatrical, Home Entertainment, and Disney Television businesses. Being on the analytics team, we handle all the reporting needs for several different clients, as well as internal teams. Our focus is to deliver weekly client reports that summarizes digital media and social campaign performance and be a primary point of contact with publishers to optimize performance of our digital ads when necessary.
